Valorant



Valorant Development began under the codename of Project A in approximately 2014, whereby Riot Games were gunning for a high-performance, competitive shooter with very low latency and tough anti-cheat.
Now Valorant gains more traction as the major esports titles and offers great global tournaments: the Valorant Champions Tour.
